# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Coordinates releases, manages release checklists, and serves as the point-of-contact for deployment-related questions.

### Responsibilities
- Own the release calendar and coordinate release windows
- Manage release checklists and pre-deployment requirements
- Coordinate with QA, DevOps, and stakeholders for go/no-go decisions
- Track release metrics and post-deployment health
- Maintain release documentation and runbooks

### Goals
- Ensure smooth, predictable releases with minimal disruption
- Reduce release-related incidents and rollbacks
- Maintain clear communication across all stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings with PM and engineering leads
- Pre-release sync with QA and DevOps
- Post-release status reports to stakeholders
- Coordination via release boards and deployment channels

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works closely with Project Managers on timeline coordination
- Partners with QA Lead to verify release readiness
- Collaborates with DevOps Engineers on deployment execution

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Ensures user-centric design, conducts usability testing, and translates requirements into intuitive interfaces.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Define interaction patterns and design systems
- Collaborate on acceptance criteria for UI/UX features
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Validate design decisions through user feedback
- Maintain design consistency across products

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings and recommendations
- Prototypes and design specs in collaboration tools
- Acceptance criteria discussions with Product Managers

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works with Product Managers to understand user needs and priorities
- Collaborates with Developers throughout implementation
- Partners with Data Analysts to measure UX impact

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
Owns test strategy, ensures test coverage and consistent quality practices, and tracks defect metrics.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test strategy and quality standards
- Ensure adequate test coverage (unit, integration, E2E)
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Track quality metrics and defect trends
- Lead bug triage and prioritization
- Verify acceptance criteria are testable

### Goals
- Maintain high product quality and reduce production defects
- Improve test automation coverage
- Enable fast, confident releases

### Typical Communication
- Test planning and coverage reviews
- Bug triage meetings
- Quality metrics reports
- Sign-off on release readiness

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Engages with Developers on testability and bug fixes
- Partners with Project Managers on quality gates and timelines
- Works with Product Managers on acceptance criteria
- Coordinates with Release Manager before releases

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Builds and maintains CI/CD pipelines, monitors infrastructure health, and automates deployment workflows.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code
- Monitor system health, performance, and alerts
- Automate deployment and rollback procedures
- Implement security scanning in build pipelines
- Manage environment configurations

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, automated deployments
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Reduce manual deployment overhead

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure changes and maintenance windows
- CI/CD pipeline updates and improvements
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Capacity planning discussions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Partners with Developers on build and deployment automation
- Collaborates with Release Managers during deployment
- Works with Security Champions on pipeline security
- Supports QA Lead with test environment management

---

## Security Champion

### Role Summary
Advocates for security best practices, reviews threat models, and runs security checks in the CI pipeline.

### Responsibilities
- Promote security awareness and best practices
- Review designs for security implications
- Conduct threat modeling sessions
- Configure and monitor security scanning tools
- Triage security findings and coordinate remediation
- Serve as liaison to central Security team

### Goals
- Integrate security throughout the development lifecycle
- Reduce security vulnerabilities in production
- Maintain compliance with security standards

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews and threat modeling sessions
- Vulnerability reports and remediation tracking
- Security training and awareness updates
- Escalation to Security team for incidents

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Collaborates with all technical roles on security practices
- Works with DevOps Engineers on pipeline security
- Partners with Developers on secure coding practices
- Coordinates with Project Managers on security requirements

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Defines success metrics, generates reports, analyzes product usage, and informs roadmap prioritization.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key success metrics
- Build dashboards and reporting tools
- Analyze user behavior and product usage
- Provide data-driven insights for prioritization
- Validate impact of features post-release
- Support A/B testing and experimentation

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Measure and communicate product impact
- Identify opportunities for improvement

### Typical Communication
- Metrics reviews and dashboard presentations
- Analysis reports and recommendations
- Experiment results and learnings
- Data requirements discussions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works closely with Product Managers on metrics and prioritization
- Partners with Project Managers during planning and post-release reviews
- Collaborates with UX Designers to measure user experience impact
- Supports Developers with instrumentation requirements
